# Institutional Ownership

The Institutional Ownership feature allows you to monitor the positions and activity of major investment firms, funds, and institutional investors. "Smart money" flows can be a leading indicator of long-term price trends.

## Features

### Top Holders
- **List of Institutions**: View the largest holders of a stock, including mutual funds, pension funds, and hedge funds.
- **Ownership %**: See what percentage of the float is held by institutions.

### Position Changes
- **Accumulation/Distribution**: Track whether institutions are adding to or reducing their positions quarter-over-quarter.
- **New Positions**: Identify when major funds take a new stake in a company.
- **Sold Out**: See when funds completely exit a position.

### Institutional Benchmarks
RealizeAlpha integrates institutional flow analysis into Trade Signals:
- **VWAP Benchmarking**: Compare price action against Volume Weighted Average Price levels often used by institutions for execution.
- **Flow Sentiment**: Gauge the aggregate sentiment of institutional activity.

## How to Access
1. Open a stock's instrument detail view.
2. Locate the **Market Intelligence** section.
3. Select **Institutional Ownership** to analyze top holders and recent changes.
