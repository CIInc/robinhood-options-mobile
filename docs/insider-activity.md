# Insider Activity

RealizeAlpha provides detailed tracking of insider transactions to help investors understand how company executives and directors are positioning themselves.

## Overview

Insider activity refers to stock transactions made by company officers, directors, and large shareholders. While insiders may sell for many reasons (tax planning, diversification), buying often signals confidence in the company's future prospects.

## Features

### Transaction Activity
- **Buy/Sell Ratios**: Visualize the balance between insider buying and selling over recent periods.
- **Transaction Types**: clear distinction between open market purchases, option exercises, and automatic sales.

### Detailed Breakdown
The Insider Activity widget provides:
- **Officer/Director Names**: See exactly who is trading.
- **Role**: Identify if the trader is a CEO, CFO, Director, or major shareholder.
- **Transaction Date**: When the trade took place.
- **Shares Traded**: The volume of the transaction.
- **Price**: The execution price.
- **Value**: Total value of the transaction.

## How to Use
1. Navigate to a specific stock's detail page.
2. Scroll to the **Market Intelligence** section.
3. Tap on **Insider Activity** to view the full dashboard.
4. Use this data to confirm technical setups or as a standalone fundamental signal.
